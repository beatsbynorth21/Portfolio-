KLAVE

An AI-powered beat marketplace built around a simple idea: in a market flooding with AI-generated music, human-made beats need a home and a way to prove it. KLAVE pairs a producer marketplace with NERO, an integrated AI assistant (powered by Claude), to help artists find and license the right sound.

ProblemAs AI-generated music floods streaming and marketplaces, producers of 100% human-made beats have no way to stand out or verify authenticity.
My RoleSolo founder & builder — product, frontend, backend, and infrastructure.
StackFirebase Auth + Firestore, Cloudinary, Vercel, Claude API (Anthropic), vanilla JS/HTML/CSS.
